About

Esco Fintech was established in 2017 to ease the burden of Government Deductors and make them tax complainant. started to work mainly in the district of Thiruvarur now extended the services to Karur, Namakkal, Virudhunagar, Ramanathapuram and Dindigul. What We Do? File TDS Returns, GST Returns, Income Tax Returns for Government Offices. How We Do? Collect data from Kalanjiyam, Prepare and File TDS Returns and GST Returns Online. When We Do? Confused with deadlines? - We are bound with IT and GST calenders to complete in time. Who We Are? Retired Employees of CTs, Treasuries, Banks and Income Tax departments etc. Where We Do? Virtually All over TamilNadu and Physically from Nannilam, Srivilliputhur and Namakkal.
